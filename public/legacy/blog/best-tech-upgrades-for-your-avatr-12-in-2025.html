Best Tech Upgrades for Your Avatr 12 in 2025

As an Avatr 12 owner, you’re already behind the wheel of a masterpiece that seamlessly blends luxury with groundbreaking electric vehicle (EV) technology. Designed for performance, comfort, and innovation, the Avatr 12 sets a high standard in the EV world.

However, as technology advances, so do the possibilities for enhancing your driving experience. The 2025 Avatr 12 has new tech upgrades, including better driving systems and faster charging. This guide helps you choose the best ones.

1. Advanced Driver Assistance Systems (ADAS)

Huawei’s Qiankun ADS 3.0

One of the standout features for the 2025 Avatr 12 car is the integration of Huawei’s Qiankun ADS 3.0 intelligent driving system. This system offers point-to-point intelligent driving, making your journeys smoother and more autonomous.

Equipped with three LiDAR units, three millimeter-wave radars, 11 cameras, and 12 ultrasonic radars, it provides comprehensive environmental sensing for enhanced safety and convenience.

Valet Parking with VPD

Navigating tight parking spaces can be challenging. The Valet Parking feature, known as VPD, enables your Avatr 12 to autonomously park in complex scenarios, including underground garages and curbside spots.

This upgrade takes the stress out of parking, allowing your vehicle to handle the task with precision.

2. Enhanced Battery and Charging Solutions

CATL’s Freevoy Super-Hybrid Battery

Battery performance is crucial for any EV owner. Upgrading to CATL’s Freevoy super-hybrid battery can significantly boost your Avatr 12’s range and efficiency. This new battery lets you drive farther on one charge.

800V SiC Platform for Fast Charging

Time is of the essence, and waiting for your vehicle to charge can be inconvenient. The 800V Silicon Carbide (SiC) platform allows your Avatr 12’s battery to charge from 30% to 80% in just 20 minutes.

This rapid charging capability ensures you’re back on the road swiftly, making long trips more feasible and enjoyable.

3. Interior Comfort and Entertainment

Rear Overhead Entertainment Screen

Passenger comfort is paramount, especially during long drives. The new rear overhead screen provides entertainment options for backseat passengers, making journeys more enjoyable for everyone.

Whether it’s streaming movies or playing games, this feature transforms the rear cabin into a multimedia hub.

Zero-Gravity Seats with Massage Function

Comfortable seating can make all the difference in your driving experience. Upgrading to zero-gravity seats in the front, which simulate the natural posture of astronauts in space, reduces fatigue during long drives.

Additionally, heated, ventilated, and massaging rear seats ensure that all passengers travel in utmost comfort.

4. Infotainment and Connectivity

HarmonyOS 4 Smart Cockpit

Staying connected is essential in today’s digital age. The HarmonyOS 4 smart cockpit offers a seamless and intuitive interface for all your infotainment needs.

With a 35.4-inch 4K integrated screen and a 15.6-inch floating central control screen, accessing navigation, media, and vehicle settings becomes a breeze.

Dual 50W Wireless Phone Charging

Keeping your devices powered is convenient with dual 50W wireless phone charging pads. This feature ensures that both the driver and passenger can charge their smartphones simultaneously without the hassle of cables, keeping you connected on the go.

5. Exterior Enhancements

Frameless Doors and Hidden Door Handles

Aesthetic upgrades can give your Avatr 12 a sleek and modern look. Frameless doors and hidden door handles not only enhance the vehicle’s appearance but also improve aerodynamics, contributing to better efficiency and a futuristic design.

Electronic Exterior Mirrors

Traditional side mirrors can be replaced with electronic exterior mirrors, offering a wider field of vision and reducing blind spots. This upgrade enhances safety and provides a modern touch to your vehicle’s exterior.

6. Performance Upgrades

Kunlun Range-Extending Power System

For those seeking to maximize their driving range, the Kunlun range-extending power system is an excellent addition. This system works in tandem with your electric motor to provide additional power when needed, ensuring you can cover longer distances without concern.

High-Performance Air Suspension Kit

Enhance your driving comfort and vehicle handling with a high-performance air suspension kit. This upgrade allows for adjustable ride height and improved shock absorption, ensuring a smoother ride across various terrains.

7. Smart Integration with the Internet of Vehicles (IoV)

HarmonyOS 4 Smart Cockpit

The latest HarmonyOS 4 Smart Cockpit offers a seamless digital experience. With a massive 35.4-inch 4K integrated display and a 15.6-inch floating central control screen, you get intuitive access to navigation, entertainment, and real-time vehicle diagnostics.

Voice-Controlled Smart Assistant

Control various functions of your Avatr 12 with simple voice commands. Whether you want to adjust the climate, navigate to a destination, or play your favorite music, the AI-powered voice assistant makes it effortless.

In a Nutshell

Upgrading your Avatr 12 with these advanced technologies not only enhances your driving experience but also keeps your vehicle at the forefront of automotive innovation. Staying updated with the latest features ensures you enjoy all the benefits that modern EV technology has to offer.

For more information on these upgrades and professional installation services, consider reaching out to EVS Electric Vehicle Services, your trusted partner in EV enhancements.

FAQ

What is the range of the Avatr 12 with the new CATL Freevoy battery?

The Avatr 12, featuring the CATL Freevoy super-hybrid battery, delivers an impressive range of up to 755 kilometers on a single charge, ensuring longer, uninterrupted journeys with enhanced efficiency.

How does Huawei's Qiankun ADS 3.0 improve driving?

Huawei’s Qiankun ADS 3.0 enables point-to-point intelligent driving, using advanced sensors to enhance safety, improve navigation, and deliver a smoother, more autonomous driving experience for Avatr 12 owners.

Can I retrofit my current Avatr 12 with the 800V SiC fast charging platform?

Retrofitting depends on your Avatr 12’s existing hardware. Consult EVS Electric Vehicle Services to evaluate compatibility and installation options for seamless integration of the 800V SiC fast charging platform.

What are the benefits of zero-gravity seats in the Avatr 12?

Zero-gravity seats minimize spinal pressure and enhance comfort on long drives by simulating a weightless posture, reducing fatigue, and providing a more relaxing & ergonomic seating experience for drivers and passengers.

Are electronic exterior mirrors legal in all regions?

Electronic exterior mirrors improve visibility, but their legality differs by region. Always verify local regulations before installation to ensure compliance and avoid potential legal or safety issues.
