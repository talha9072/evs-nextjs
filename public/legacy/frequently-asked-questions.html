Frequently Asked Questions

Where is EVS located?

EVS operates across multiple locations including Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Ajman, Al Ain in the United Arab Emirates, and Riyadh in Saudi Arabia. Our workshops are fully equipped and conveniently located to serve electric vehicle owners throughout the region.

What types of electric vehicles do you service?

We service a wide range of electric vehicles, including brands like Tesla, BYD, Zeekr, Rox, Avatr and other full-electric and hybrid models.

Do you only work on electric vehicles?

Yes. EVS specializes exclusively in electric vehicles, with technicians, tools, and systems dedicated to EV-specific repairs, diagnostics, and maintenance. Click here to see our services.

Can I bring my EV to EVS after an accident?

Absolutely. We offer full bodywork, panel replacement, structural alignment, and insurance support—including damage estimation and claim processing. Click here for more information on insurance services.

Do you handle both mechanical and electrical repairs?

Yes. We provide complete mechanical and electrical services, from brake and suspension work to high-voltage battery repairs, key programming, and firmware updates.

Do you work with insurance companies?

Yes. We assist with accident claims, damage documentation, and direct billing with most major UAE insurance providers.

Do you offer diagnostics and regular checkups?

Yes. EVS provides comprehensive diagnostics for low- and high-voltage systems, as well as full mechanical checkups for EV health and performance tracking.

What makes EVS different from regular auto repair shops?

EVS is built specifically for electric vehicles. We use EV-compatible tools, follow high-voltage safety standards, and train our team in EV-only platforms and technologies. Learn more about EVS

Can I book an appointment online?

Yes. You can contact us here to schedule diagnostics, repairs, or consultations.

Do you offer genuine EV parts?

We source high-quality, EV-compatible parts that meet or exceed manufacturer specifications for reliability and safety.
