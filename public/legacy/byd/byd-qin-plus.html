About BYD QIN Plus.

The BYD Qin Plus is a sleek plug-in hybrid sedan that blends performance, efficiency, and smart technology. Equipped with a 1.5L engine and an electric motor, it delivers a combined output of 307 hp and 325 Nm of torque, accelerating from 0 to 100 km/h in approximately 7.3 seconds. Its 18.3 kWh Blade Battery offers up to 120 km of pure electric range, with a total driving range exceeding 1,200 km . Inside, the Qin Plus features a 10.1-inch rotatable touchscreen with Apple CarPlay and Android Auto, providing seamless connectivity . At EVS UAE, we specialize in servicing advanced EVs like the BYD Qin Plus, ensuring its high-voltage systems and intelligent features are expertly maintained.

Frequently Asked Questions

What maintenance does the BYD Qin Plus typically require?

The Qin Plus often requires updates to its hybrid-electric systems, battery service, and brake component checks.

Is your service center capable of handling BYD Qin Plus hybrid systems?

Yes, we are fully capable of diagnosing and repairing the Qin Plus's dual powertrain (electric + combustion) systems.

Can you fix charging or range issues in the BYD Qin Plus?

Absolutely. We address range concerns by evaluating battery condition, drive efficiency, and system calibration.

Do you offer hybrid system diagnostics for BYD Qin Plus DM-i models?

Yes, we are experienced in servicing BYD DM-i models, offering in-depth analysis and repairs for hybrid-specific components.
