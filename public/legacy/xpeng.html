Frequently Asked Questions

Do you service all XPENG models in the UAE?

Yes, at EVS we service all XPENG models available in the region, including the XPENG G7, G9, and other new releases. Our technicians are trained to handle diagnostics, maintenance, and repairs specific to XPENG vehicles.

Is XPENG maintenance different from regular car servicing?

Yes, XPENG vehicles are electric and require specialized care. Unlike traditional cars, they do not need oil changes but do require battery health checks, software updates, and system diagnostics. We use XPENG-compatible tools and procedures to ensure accurate service.

Do you offer XPENG warranty-related repairs?

Absolutely. EVS is equipped to carry out warranty-related servicing and repairs on XPENG vehicles, in accordance with manufacturer guidelines.

How often should I service my XPENG EV in the UAE?

We recommend servicing your XPENG every 12 months or 20,000 km, whichever comes first. However, some checks, like software updates and tire inspections, may be needed more frequently depending on driving habits and UAE climate conditions.

Where in the UAE do you offer XPENG servicing?

We serve XPENG EV owners across the UAE — including Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Ajman, and Al Ain. Our service center is equipped to support clients from all emirates.

Can I get a software update for my XPENG vehicle at EVS?

Yes, we offer software update services for XPENG models to ensure your vehicle benefits from the latest performance, safety, and feature enhancements.

Do you offer pickup and drop-off for XPENG servicing?

Yes, we provide convenient pickup and drop-off options for XPENG owners across the UAE, subject to availability. Please contact our service team to arrange this in advance.
