Privacy Policy – EVS UAE

Your privacy is important to us. This page explains how EVS UAE collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our website and services.

At EVS UAE, we are committed to maintaining the trust and confidence of our visitors. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your personal data when you interact with our website, and the rights you have regarding your information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Data collected through Facebook/Meta Pixel for marketing and remarketing purposes.
  • Analytics data via Google Analytics and Google Tag Manager to monitor traffic, engagement, and improve our site performance.
  • Personal details (such as name, phone number, and email) voluntarily provided by you through forms or direct contact.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data may be used to:

  • Improve the usability, performance, and content of our website.
  • Provide a personalized and relevant experience for visitors.
  • Serve targeted advertisements across platforms like Facebook and Google.
  • Communicate with you regarding inquiries, services, or updates.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share certain data with trusted service providers such as analytics or advertising platforms, strictly for the purpose of enhancing our services.

4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our site uses cookies and tracking technologies to enhance user experience, analyze traffic, and deliver tailored ads. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time.

5. Data Retention & Security

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to provide services or as required by law. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access or misuse.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information. You may also opt out of analytics tracking or marketing communications by contacting us directly.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Phone: 600 500 387
  • Email: info@evsuae.com

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Please check this page periodically for updates.
