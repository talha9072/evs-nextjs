Car Air Conditioning System Services

AC Refrigerant Recharge

Low refrigerant levels? We recharge your EV’s AC with eco-friendly refrigerant to restore cool air, perfect for Dubai’s hot climate.

Compressor Performance Check

A failing compressor reduces cooling. We inspect and repair your AC compressor to ensure efficient operation and comfort.

Cabin Filter Replacement

Dirty filters affect air quality. We replace cabin filters to improve airflow, remove odors, and keep your EV’s interior fresh.

AC Leak Detection

Warm air from your AC? We detect and seal leaks in the system to prevent refrigerant loss and maintain consistent cooling.

Blower Motor Repair

Weak airflow? We repair or replace the blower motor to ensure strong, even air distribution throughout your EV’s cabin.

Full AC System Diagnostics

We check the compressor, refrigerant, filters, and vents to diagnose issues, ensuring your EV’s AC keeps you cool and comfortable.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I have my EV's air conditioning system serviced?

Regularly service your electric vehicle’s air conditioning system every 12 months or after every 20,000 kilometers. This ensures optimal efficiency, maintains performance, & prevents costly major issues.

What are the signs that my EV's air conditioning needs servicing?

Experiencing reduced cooling, unusual noises, unpleasant odors, inconsistent temperatures, or delayed cooling clearly indicates that your system requires professional servicing.

Is EV air conditioning maintenance different from traditional vehicles?

Yes, electric vehicles need specialized tools and expertise for their unique components and drivetrain integration, which our certified technicians expertly handle.

How long does an EV air conditioning service take?

Routine maintenance typically takes one to two hours, while complex repairs or extensive overhauls may require up to half a day to ensure thorough & efficient completion.

Does EV air conditioning service affect the vehicle warranty?

No, EVS UAE meticulously follows manufacturer guidelines, ensuring your vehicle’s warranty remains intact while providing expert and reliable maintenance services.

What brands do you specialize in for EV air conditioning service?

We specialize in servicing BYD, Avatar, Zeekar, Rox, and other popular electric vehicle brands, providing expert care and comprehensive support to all our customers.

Are EV air conditioning systems more energy-efficient?

Yes, they employ state-of-the-art technology to significantly reduce energy consumption, thereby extending battery life and substantially improving overall efficiency.

Are EV air conditioning parts more expensive than traditional components?

While EV AC parts may be more expensive, their superior quality ensures excellent value, enhanced durability, and long-lasting performance for your vehicle.

Do you serve EV air conditioning service customers from Abu Dhabi?

Absolutely, we proudly serve customers in Dubai and Abu Dhabi, delivering exceptional services tailored specifically for residents of Abu Dhabi.

What preventive measures can I take to maintain my EV's AC system?

Replace filters, verify refrigerant levels, clean the system, and prevent overuse by scheduling regular maintenance with EVS UAE for reliable performance.

How do you ensure the environmental friendliness of your AC services?

We consistently use eco-friendly refrigerants and strictly follow proper waste management protocols, effectively minimizing our environmental impact and promoting sustainability.
